Nuoc mam (Broken rice fish sauce)


This flavouring sauce is found widely accompanying almost all meals across Vietnam. This jazzed up sauce
can be used to add flavor to bland rice or as a dipping sauce for spring rolls. It can be added to any dish.
The base of the flavor is the umami tinge of the fish sauce and garlic, with a balancing sweetness of the coconut
water or sugar. The minced garlic and chilli adds depth.

The ingredients

  • Fish sauce (2 tbsp)
  • Luke warm water or coconut water (6 tbsp)
  • Lime (1 1/2 tbsp)
  • Sugar (2 tbsp)
  • Garlic (1 clove minced)
  • chilli (1 thai chilli finely sliced)

Instructions

  1. Dissolve sugar in water (or coconut water). Heat small portion of water to dissolve sugar in. Cooling it with the remaining water.
  2. Add the lime juice untill it taste like lemonade
  3. Add fish sauce in small increments. Keep oin mind it can be a little strong. It will be used to flavour
    bland food.
  4. Add garlic and chillies. Give it a stir. Enjoy.
