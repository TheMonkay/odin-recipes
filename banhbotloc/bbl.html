Banh bot loc (Clear shrimp dumplings)


This gelatinous snack (sometimes meal) has a fun sticky and chewy texture with savoury bits.
It is enjoyed on its own or with nuoc mam.

Ingredients - for 30 - 40 pcs

  • fresh banana leaves - 1 kg
  • Pork belly - 200 g
  • Sugar - 1 tsp
  • Salt - 1 3/4 tsp
  • Fish sauce - 1 tbsp
  • Garlic - 1 clove, minced
  • Shrimp - 300 g
  • Cooking oil - 1 tbs
  • Tapioca - 400 g
  • Water - 600 mL
  • Shallots - 3, minced

Prepare the banana leaves

  1. Cut squares with the siz of 12 x 18 cm out of the banana leaves.
  2. Blanch the banana leaf squares in boiling water for 1 - 2 minutes. Let them drain and cool before drying
    them with a clean cloth.

Prepare the filling

The pork

  1. Cut the pork into 3 x 5 cm strips.
  2. In a large ball combine sugar (1 tsp), salt (1/4 tsp), fish sauce (1 tbs) and minced garlic (1 tbsp).
  3. Mix the pork into the mixture you have just made. Let it marinate for at least 2 hours.

The Shrimp

  1. Clean the shrimp by removing their heads and taili. (you can leave on the shell of the shrimp)
  2. Take your cleaned shrimp and mix in salt (1 tbsp) and let it marinade in the fridge for 2 hours.

Cook the filling

  1. After marination, warm up oil in a pan and quickly stir fry the remaining garlic and shallots on a low heat. Then add the pork and shrimp. Cook and season to preference.

Prepare the batter

  1. In a bowl, mix tapioca and 200 mL of water till smooth consistency.
  2. At the same time, bring 400 mL water to boil and add salt (1/2 tsp) and oil (1 tbsp). Turn down the heat once you add the tapioca mixture.
  3. Poor the tapioca mixture into the boiling water, continue stirring until the mixture thickens but still has a milky
    white colour. Immediately turn off the heat and continue to stir to smooth the batter.

Cook the dumplings


  1. Lightly oil a quarter of a banana leaf square. Divide the dough in equal parts and place the dough in a corner of a banana leaf (as in picture). Flatten the dough
    and place the pork and shrimp onto the dough. Wrap the the dough and filling close in the banana leaf.
  2. Place the rolled up dough in banana leaves in a steamer and steam for 15-20 minutes. Enjoy with nuoc mam.
